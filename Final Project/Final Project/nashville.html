Nashville is Music City!

    Nashville has diverse industry, history, and culture.
  • Nashville has many major industries from across the modern business climate.
  • Nashville was founded a few hundred years ago in 1779. Talk about wisdom!
  • Nashville is a diverse blend of many kinds of people. The parthenon, fine art, and Tennessee Performing Art Center are all great ways to get your cultural fix.

Nashville brings modern collaboration for business and liesure together with an old fashioned feel.

Some important industries in Nashville are Health Care, Finance, Auto Workers, Insurance, Music Production, Publishing Print Materials, and tourism. Nashville has an incredible history as the state capital of tennessee. The population, as of 2020, was just under 700,000 people. The city is located in the Middle Tenneessee grand dvision. Nashville is an urban city. The median income for a household as of 2020 was 46,000 per year. In 1779, Nashville was founded by James Robertson. The city was originally called Fort Nashborough named after the Revolutionary War hero Francis Nash. Nashville rapidly expanded in the following decades beause of easy river access and central location. In 1806 Nashville officially became a city and in 1843 it became the Capital of Tennessee. Over the following decades the city thrived.
