The population of memphis is just over 650,000 as of 2020. Modern day memphis was founded in 1819. Memphis is located in the West Tennessee Grand Division. The city is urban. The median income was 32,000 per household as of 2020. Memphis has a diverse range of industry which provides plenty of opportunity for you and your family to start a new life in a beautiful place. Information technology, auto manufacturing and supply, advanced manufacturing, finance, healthcare and life science are all large industries around Memphis. The city is located on the Western edge of the State and is bordered by the Mississippi river. After its intial settlement in 1819, Memphis quickly grew into a major city due to its flat, easily accessible terrain and excellent accesss to one of the most important rivers in early American history.

Fun facts about Memphis!
  1. Memphis was the larget city in Tennessee for almost all of the states history only to be recently supplanted by Nashville due to an influx of relocations from other states since the pandemic.

  2. Memphis is the site of the national Civil Rights Museum.

  3. Elvis Presley's home, Graceland, is in Memphis and attracts half a million visitors annually.

  4. Memphis has both a zoo and aquarium for tourists and locals alike!

  5. Popular blues artist W.C. Handy got his start on Beale street in Memphis giving birth to the blues genre.
