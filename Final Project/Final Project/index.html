Tennessee is the place to be!

    Tennessee has 3 geographical regions called "Grand Divisions"
  • East Tennessee
  • Middle Tennessee
  • West Tennessee

Tennessee's three "Grand Divisions" are both cultural and geographic in nature.

East Tennessee includes the Great Smoky Mountains, the eastern Tennessee River valley, and a portion of the Cumberland Plateau. Middle Tennessee includes the rest of the Cumberland Plateau and the low-lying Central basin, completely surrounded by the Highland Rim. This are of Tennessee is hilly and fertile. West Tennessee ends with the Mississippi river and the Tennessee River valley and is primarily comprised of low flat lands. In the 1835 acts of Tennessee, a legal distinction between the "Grand Divisions" was also specified. Under that law, the surpreme court had to meet once at the capital of each region once each year.

Fun facts about the great state!
  1. Tennessee is the birthplace and home of PepsiCo's Mountain Dew. Drink up!

  2. Nashville's Grand Ole Opry is the longest running radio show of any kind. Shoutout country music fans!

  3. There is some historical evidence that cotton candy was first spun up in Tennessee. Yummy.

  4. Great Smokey Mountain National Park is the most visited park in the country by far more than doubling Yellowstone's annual total. If you have not made the climb... go.

  5. America's first female Senator, Hattie Carraway was born in Tennessee in 1878. That is excellent heritage.
